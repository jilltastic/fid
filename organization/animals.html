Aldo


Meet Aldo! Aldo is very friendly dolphin. He loves to swim laps and play catch. Aldo is our youngest dolphin on site. He is in progress of learning a few tricks. Come meet Aldo and watch him swim!

Marshy

Meet Marshy! Marshy is our largest animal on site. She will literally eat ALL day! Marshy is very sweet and loves to meet new friends. Come meet Marshy today and be her new friend!

Polka

Meet Polka! Polka is very shy when it comes to meeting new people. She gets her name from her spots (obviously). She loves when meal time comes around. Come meet and feed Polka!
